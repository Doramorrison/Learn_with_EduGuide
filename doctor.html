Top 3 Easiest Countries to Apply for scholarship

IN ASIA

China
India
Malaysia

Why It's easy?

China : Offers abundant scholarships like the Chinese Government Scholarship (CSC), covering tuition, accommodation, and living costs. Many programs are available in English.

India : Scholarships such as the ICCR focus on academic merit and cultural exchange, with a cost-effective education system.

Malaysia : Scholarships like the Malaysia International Scholarship (MIS) are well-funded and support international students with clear application processes.
