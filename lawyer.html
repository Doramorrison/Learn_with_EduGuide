Top 5 famous Universities for Law

IN ASIA

National University of Singapore (NUS)

A top-ranked university offering world-class law programs with a focus on international law, corporate law, and legal research.

The University of Tokyo – Japan

Renowned for its legal studies, it offers programs that emphasize constitutional law, civil law, and Japan's unique legal system.

Peking University – China

Known for its prestigious law school, it provides rigorous training in Chinese and international law with a global perspective.

Hong Kong University (HKU) – Hong Kong

HKU excels in law education, focusing on commercial law, human rights law, and legal practice in Asia.

Seoul National University (SNU) – South Korea

SNU offers highly respected law programs specializing in Korean law, constitutional law, and global legal studies.

Top 5 famous Universities for Law

IN EUROPE

University of Oxford – United Kingdom

One of the world’s most prestigious institutions, Oxford offers exceptional law programs focusing on legal theory, international law, and human rights.

University of Cambridge

Cambridge is renowned for its rigorous legal education, emphasizing constitutional law, corporate law, and global legal systems.

Leiden University – Netherlands

Famous for its international law programs, Leiden University offers expertise in European law, public international law, and human rights.

Sorbonne University (Paris 1)

A historic institution, Sorbonne specializes in civil law, European law, and legal research, with a strong focus on French legal traditions.

University of Amsterdam

Known for its focus on European Union law, international law, and global justice, it combines academic excellence with practical training.

Top 3 Easiest Countries to Apply for scholarship

IN ASIA

Singapore
South Korea
China

Why It's easy?

Singapore: Offers fully-funded government scholarships like the NUS and NTU programs. Applications focus on academic merit and are simple, online, and transparent.

South Korea: GKS provides tuition, living, and travel costs, with clear guidelines and embassy or university-based applications.

China: CGS covers tuition, accommodation, and stipends. The process is straightforward, through embassies or universities, with wide availability.

Top 3 Easiest Countries to Apply for Scholarships

IN EUROPE

Why It's easy?

Germany: DAAD Scholarships and tuition-free public universities make education affordable. Applications require only academic records and motivation letter.

United Kingdom: Chevening Scholarships cover all costs and focus on leadership potential, with a clear application system.

Netherland: Scholarships like NL High Potential offer full or partial funding, with merit-based applications emphasizing academic excellence.
