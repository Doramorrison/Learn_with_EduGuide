ENTREPRENEUR

Business Studies

Provides knowledge of business operations, financial management, and entrepreneurship. It teaches key concepts like market analysis, business planning, and financial forecasting.

Economics

Helps understand how markets function, supply and demand, and economic factors that impact businesses. A solid understanding of economics is crucial for making informed business decisions.

Mathematics

Essential for managing finances, calculating profits, understanding statistics, and working with data. It strengthens problem-solving skills that are crucial for entrepreneurship.

English Language

Strong communication skills are vital for any entrepreneur. English helps develop writing, reading, and presentation skills necessary for pitching ideas, negotiating, and managing teams.

Accounting

Teaches how to manage finances, understand financial statements, and track expenses. Accounting is fundamental for ensuring a business is financially healthy.

ICT

In today’s digital world, ICT skills are critical for running an online business, using business management software, and understanding cybersecurity.

Top 3 Easiest Countries to Apply for scholarship

IN ASIA

South Korea
Singapore
Malaysia

Why It's easy?

South Korea : Scholarships are widely available through universities and government programs. The simple application process and strong support for entrepreneurship make it easy for international students.

Singapore : The government and universities offer clear scholarships, particularly in business and entrepreneurship, with a focus on attracting global talent and fostering innovation.

Malaysia : With affordable education and scholarships like MIS, Malaysia supports international students through mentorship and startup programs, making the process accessible.
